Moving on (1 - 4 years)

Our Moving On program is designed to support children as they transition from infant care to more structured preschool activitis. This program focuses on developing independence, social skills, and cognitive abilities in a fun and engaging environment. The following is a typical daily schedule for our Moving On program:

13:00 - 14:00 Snack Time
14:00 - 15:00 Changing Nappy
15:00 - 15:30 Refreshment
15:30 - 16:00 Departure
