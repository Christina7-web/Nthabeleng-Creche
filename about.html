About Us

Our Story

Nthabeleng Creche was founded in 2012 by Gladys Marima, a passionate early childhood educator with a vision to create a nurturing and stimulating environment for young children.

We believe that every child is unique and deserves the opportunity to learn and grow at their own pace.

Our Values

  • Nurturing: Providing a safe and loving environment.
  • Education: Fostering a love of learning.
  • Creativity: Encouraging self-expression and imagination.
  • Community: Building strong relationships with families.

Our Team
