How to Enroll

We are excited you are considering Nthabeleng Creche for your child! Please follow these steps to enroll:

  1. Inquiry: Contact us to schedule a visit and preliminary chat. Call us at +27828655825 or Contact us on Whatsapp
  2. Tour: Explore our facilities and meet our team. We'll show you around and answer any questions you may have.
  3. Application & Enrollment Confirmation: If you are happy to proceed after the tour, complete our application form in person. You will receive immediate confirmation of your child's enrollment status upon submission of the application.
  4. Prepare for your child's Exciting Journey!: Once confirmed, we will provide you with all the necessary information to prepare for your child's first day.

For more information or to schedule a visit, please contact us:

Phone: +27828655825

Alternatively: Contact us on Whatsapp
